1. To verify that Kruise has started, run:

  kubectl --namespace=kruise-system get pods -l control-plane=controller-manager

Wait for pods turning into Running, and one of them should become Ready.

2. List all CRDs installed by Kruise:

  kubectl get crd | grep kruise.io

3. You can read the docs for further information.

  https://github.com/openkruise/kruise/tree/master/docs
